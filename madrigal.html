12th Annual Midwinter Madrigal Feast

November 16 and 17, 2012
At Summit on 16th
Doors open: 7:00pm
Performance Starts: 7:30pm

The Council for the Medieval and Renaissance Faire and the Ohio State Medieval and Renaissance Performer's Guild would like to announce the 12th Annual Midwinter Madrigal Feast. We are pleased to announce that due to your support, and to your increasing numbers, Madrigal will have a two night run. The event will take place November 16 and 17 at Summit on 16th (http://summitumc.org/). Doors will open at 7PM on both nights.

Madrigal is a dinner theatre experience which features a fun and entertaining night of medieval and renaissance dancing, songs, music, juggling, fighting as well as several other talents. Come in from the cold and join us for an evening of food and festivities!

Four appetizer options are available. Our bread platter is a humble half-loaf of bread with a herb mix and olive oil for dipping. The sausage platter plates summer sausage with crackers. The fruit platter contains a variety of seasonal fruits, and the vegetable platter contains a seasonal mix of vegetables.

For dessert, we offer two cakes: the Oreo Chocolate Cake, a rich chocolate cake topped with an Oreo flavored buttercream frosting, and the Mozart Torte, light white cake with strawberries and icing in the center, topped with strawberries under a clear glaze.
